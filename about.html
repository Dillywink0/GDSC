About Us

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are university-based community groups run by Google Developers for students with an interest in growing as developers. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Our Mission

Help you become well-rounded Software Engineers, not just Software Developers Teach practical technical skills using Google Technologies Host Tech Talks with industry professionals Run hackathons and other fun social events Cover the basics of Business and Entrepreneurship Coach teams for the annual Google Solution Challenge competition Work with community groups and local government to make a real difference in our local community
